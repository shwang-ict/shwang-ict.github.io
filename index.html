Welcome

Shuhui Wang is a professor in the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He received his bachelor degree from Tsinghua University in 2006 and Ph.D degree from the Institute of Computing Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences. He has authored or coauthored more than 80 academic papers in prestigious international journals, such as TPAMI, TIP, TKDE, TMM and top-level international conferences, such as NeurIPS, CVPR, ICCV, ECCV, ACMMM, SIGMOD, VLDB. He is the reviewer of various international journals and conferences. He has served as area chair for ACM Multimedia many times and participanted in the organization of many international conferences.

His research areas include cross-media analytics and trustable machine learning.

Internship and Graduate Students (both Ph.D. and Master) Opening: If you are interested in doing research on cross-media analytics, trustable machine learning and computer vision, please send detailed CV to me.
